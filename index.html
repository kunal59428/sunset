About Deen Dayal Society

Sunsat Group proudly presents Deen Dayal Society, a 30+ acre integrated township on Garhi Bolni Road, Sector 25, Rewari. This meticulously planned development offers the perfect blend of modern living and natural serenity.

Our DDAY-approved project features thoughtfully designed residential plots, commercial spaces for shops and offices, and lush landscaped green spaces that promote a healthy lifestyle.

The prime location on Garhi Bolni Road ensures excellent connectivity to Delhi NCR and Rewari city, making it an ideal choice for both residential and investment purposes.
